25.02.2016 Reflections

What's your take on the DBC / eda experience? What are your impressions?

I like how forward thinking and holistic the program is.

I like the idea, which came up a lot in the Shereef video, about changing your mindset. I am happy to get out of the 'consumer mindset' and in to a 'creator mindset'. This seems like a great idea.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I see myself doing my very best to engage with all aspects of the course. Typically, in study or work, i prefer to forge ahead alone, working independently. I know that the eda revolves around a lot of pairing or group work, so that will be new.

Have your expectations of eda changed? If so, how?

My expectations have not changed at all. I spent a lot of time talking to different people about eda, so i think my expectations were very realistic.

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

yes excited, and no i am not nervous
